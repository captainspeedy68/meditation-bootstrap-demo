Meditation Techniques
Upasana Vipassana Mindfulness Transcendental Loving-Kindness Zen Meditation

Upasana Meditation

A devotional form of meditation practiced in traditions like Sri Vaishnavism. Focus on connecting with the divine through rituals, chanting, and visualization of the Lord’s attributes.

Vipassana Meditation

A practice of self-transformation through self-observation. Focus on deep insight into the true nature of reality, using mindfulness of breath and bodily sensations.

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on your breath and the present moment. Observe your thoughts without judgment and let them pass like clouds in the sky.

Transcendental Meditation

Use a mantra to transcend distractions and reach a state of deep relaxation and inner calm.

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Cultivate an attitude of compassion and love by repeating phrases like "May I be happy, may you be safe."

Zen Meditation

Practice seated meditation (zazen) with a focus on posture, breathing, and mindfulness, often in silence.
